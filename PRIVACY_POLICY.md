# Privacy Policy for Ytp Downloader

**Last Updated: December 9, 2025**

## Overview

Ytp Downloader ("the Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Application handles your information when you use our desktop software.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect

Ytp Downloader is designed with privacy in mind. We **DO NOT**:

- Collect or transmit any personal information to external servers
- Track your browsing history or download history remotely
- Require user accounts or registration
- Use analytics or telemetry services
- Share any data with third parties
- Store your data on cloud servers

### Information Stored Locally

The Application stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

1. **Application Settings**
   - Download directory preferences
   - Quality and format preferences
   - Theme preferences (dark/light mode)
   - Interface language settings

2. **Download History**
   - URLs of downloaded videos
   - Download timestamps
   - File paths and formats
   - Download status (completed, failed, etc.)

3. **Application Logs**
   - Error logs for troubleshooting
   - Download operation logs
   - Application performance data

All this data remains on your local device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

## How We Use Your Information

The locally stored information is used solely to:

- Remember your preferences between sessions
- Display your download history within the Application
- Troubleshoot issues and improve application stability
- Prevent duplicate downloads of the same content

## Third-Party Services

### YouTube

When you use the Application to download content, the Application communicates directly with YouTube's servers to:
- Retrieve video information and metadata
- Download video and audio streams

These communications are subject to [YouTube's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms).

### FFmpeg

The Application uses FFmpeg for video and audio processing. FFmpeg runs entirely on your local device and does not transmit any data externally.

## Data Storage and Security

- All application data is stored in standard configuration directories on your device
- Download history and settings are stored in plain text or JSON format
- You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time
- No encryption is applied as no sensitive personal information is collected

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

### Access Your Data
- Settings: Located in the application's configuration directory
- Download History: Accessible through the History tab in the Application
- Logs: Located in the application's log directory

### Delete Your Data
You can delete your data by:
- Clearing download history through the Application interface
- Manually deleting configuration files from your system
- Uninstalling the Application (removes all associated data)

### Export Your Data
- Download history can be exported from the History interface
- Configuration files are in human-readable formats (JSON/text)

## Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect any information from children. As we do not collect personal information from any users, the Application can be used by individuals of any age, subject to parental guidance and YouTube's own age restrictions.

## Changes to Downloaded Content

**Important**: Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to download and use content from YouTube. The Application is a tool and does not grant any rights to copyrighted material. Users must:

- Respect copyright laws and YouTube's Terms of Service
- Only download content they have permission to download
- Use downloaded content in accordance with applicable laws
